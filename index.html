About

Indian Overseas Bank (IOB) is one of India's leading public sector banks. It provides a wide range of banking and financial services to individuals, businesses, farmers, and corporate customers.It was established with a strong focus on international banking and foreign exchange services.

Why is it called "Indian Overseas Bank"?

The bank was established to specialize in foreign exchange and overseas banking. It began operations simultaneously in Karaikudi, Chennai, and Rangoon (now Yangon, Myanmar), making it one of the first Indian banks with an international presence.

Mission

To provide reliable, customer-focused banking services while supporting India's economic growth through modern and digital banking solutions.
